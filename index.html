About us

Naga City Science High School is a secondary public science high school located in the Heart of Bicol, Naga City.

Headed by Ma'am Baby Ruby F. Laurente, Sayans (as it is affectionately called) has provided quality science-focused education to its students since 1994; 3 decades of service for God and man.
